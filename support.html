Frequently Asked Questions

Does Pet Doc Pro offer a free trial?

Yes. Eligible users receive a 3-day free trial with full access. You can cancel anytime during the trial and will not be charged.

What subscription plans are available?

Pet Doc Pro offers three subscription options:

  • Starter: $4.99/month – 1 pet
  • Pro: $9.99/month or $109.89/year – 1 pet
  • Family: $14.99/month or $164.89/year – unlimited pets

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, select Pet Doc Pro, and tap “Cancel Subscription.” Access continues until the end of the billing period.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Sign in using the same Apple ID and tap Restore Purchases inside the app.

Is my pet’s information secure?

Yes. Pet Doc Pro uses industry-standard security practices. We do not sell personal or pet data.

Does Pet Doc Pro provide medical advice?

No. Pet Doc Pro is a record-keeping and organization tool only. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical care.

Billing & Subscription Details

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. Apple manages all billing and refunds.
