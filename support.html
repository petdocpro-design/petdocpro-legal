? Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new pet?

Tap the "+" button on the Pets tab to add a new pet. Fill in your pet's details including name, species, breed, and photo. You can always edit this information later.

How do I set up medication reminders?

Go to your pet's profile, tap "Add Reminder," and select the type (medication, vaccine, vet visit, etc.). Set the date, time, and frequency. You'll receive notifications at your scheduled times.

Can I use Pet Doc Pro for multiple pets?

Yes! With our Unlimited Pets plan, you can add and manage as many pets as you have. The 1 Pet plan allows you to manage one pet with full features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Pet Doc Pro, and tap "Cancel Subscription." Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Is my pet's data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never sell your information to third parties. Your pet's health records remain private and secure.

How do I generate a lost pet flyer?

Open your pet's profile and tap "Lost Pet Flyer." Add your contact information, and a professional flyer will be generated instantly. You can share it digitally or print it.

Can I restore my data on a new device?

Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID on your new device, and your subscription and data will be restored automatically.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains on your device. If you resubscribe, you'll have full access again. We recommend exporting important records before canceling.
