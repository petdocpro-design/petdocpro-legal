Subscription Plans

Pet Doc Pro offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions to unlock advanced pet management features.

Starter Plan

  • $4.99 per month
  • Track one pet with full access to all app features

Family Plan

  • $14.99 per month
  • Track multiple pets
  • Family Sharing support

Both subscription plans include a 3-day free trial. After the trial period ends, your Apple ID account will be charged unless the subscription is canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the trial.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID Account Settings after purchase.

Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription.
